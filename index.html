WHAT IS AIRED

We believe in keep dancing. We believe in snow in Sapporo. We believe in the distant sound of the ancient bell, knight leader, sheep with a star mark, Dolphin Hotel.

We believe in the little people that star fire turns into.

We believe in the boundless blue sky and grassland, believe in the wind, believe in the city above the sky, believe in the train on the sea, believe in never looking back, believe in the Totoro bus, believe in the heron, believe in the boy, believe in the "Puma", believe in youth. Believe in cynicism that is not cynicism, believe in humanity, believe in the beast, believe in the feeling of freedom, believe in the end, believe in prayer, believe in the burning of fire.
